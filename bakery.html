Our Drinks

There are our top drinks That We Make In Ramala Cafe.

Freanch Coffee

French coffee evokes images of Parisian cafes and leisurely mornings. While it might conjure thoughts of a specific brewing method or bean origin, the essence lies in the experience. It's about savoring a small cup of strong, dark roast coffee, often espresso-based, in a relaxed setting. Whether enjoyed black or with a touch of milk, French coffee embodies a cultural tradition, a moment of indulgence, and a connection to a timeless European lifestyle.

Cappuccino

A cappuccino is a harmonious blend of espresso, steamed milk, and a velvety layer of foam. This classic Italian coffee drink offers a balanced and indulgent experience. The espresso provides a rich, bold flavor, while the steamed milk adds a creamy sweetness. The microfoam, created by expertly steaming the milk, provides a silky texture and a beautiful design. Often topped with a dusting of cocoa powder or cinnamon, a cappuccino is a delightful treat for coffee lovers seeking a sophisticated and satisfying brew.

Espresso

Espresso, a concentrated coffee beverage, is a cornerstone of coffee culture. It's crafted by forcing hot water through finely-ground coffee beans under high pressure. This process yields a small, powerful shot with a rich, complex flavor. The ideal espresso features a dark, oily surface known as crema, which adds a layer of sweetness and depth to the drink. This bold and aromatic beverage serves as the foundation for many popular coffee drinks, including cappuccinos, lattes, and americanos.
